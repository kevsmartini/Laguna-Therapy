TMJ Massage

This type of massage is designed to address issues related to the temporomandibular joint (TMJ). The temporomandibular joint connects the jaw to the skull and is essential for everyday activities such as talking, chewing, and swallowing. Temporomandibular joint massage focuses on relieving tension, pain, and dysfunction associated with this area.

Reflexology

It is a therapeutic practice based on the idea that there are reflex areas on the feet, hands, and ears that are connected to different parts of the body. By applying pressure to these reflex zones, specific responses can be stimulated in the corresponding organs and systems, thereby promoting overall health and well-being.
